EXPRESSION OF GRATITUDE TOWARDS GOD 1: ACCEPTABLE WORSHIP

LESSON 14

3RD DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT

2 Samuel 24:18-25

MEMORY VERSE

John 4:24 - "God is a spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in Spirit and in truth".

CENTRAL TRUTH

Worshipping God in an acceptable way is a form of showing gratitude to Him.

INTRODUCTION

WHAT IS ACCEPTABLE WORSHIP?

Acceptable worship is the acceptance of the reverence given to one from another typical example is an Oba in the Yoruba tribe who has people eulogizing them. The stretch of their sceptre shows the acceptance oftheir subject's worship.


An example from Scriptures is the story of King Ahasuerus and Queen Esther, 5:1-2.

LIFE APPLICATION: Check the intent of your gratitude; is it to spite others? Is it to
please others? Is it to please God? Is it to ascribe glory to yourself or to God? Your worship
must be you returning all glory to God for it to be acceptable.

CONCLUSION: do you worship and offer gratitude to God? Is it really unto the
Lord or for eye service? God accepts worship that is made genuinely from a pure heart.
Offer acceptable worship to God; acceptable worship can only come from a grateful heart.
