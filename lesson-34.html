BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
DIVINE PROTECTION

LESSON 34

21ST APRIL

BIBLE TEXT

Psalm 91

MEMORY VERSE

Psalm 91:1 - "He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty."

CENTRAL TRUTH

God is more than able to protect you when you have a grateful heart.

INTRODUCTION

Divine protection is God's way of supernaturally shielding us from
harm or danger. Many of us recited Psalm 91 regularly during the pandemic; why? Ps 22:3
makes us understand that God inhabits the praises of His people. This means that God's
presence comes down when we express our gratitude to Him. When we are in His
presence, we are protected. Spirit if you do not have the Holy Spirit.

LIFE APPLICATION: All the benefits listed above will be ours when we are always
thankful to God. There is no true protection anywhere else except from God Almighty.
Some joined cults or secret societies for protection and they got killed at the end - fake
protection. However, God's protection is very sure if you are His child.

with it a promise of divine protection, for God says, "Because he hath set his love upon
me, therefore will I deliver him" (Ps. 91:14).
