GOD THE FATHER DELIGHTS IN A
GRATEFUL HEART

LESSON 23

4TH FEBRUARY

BIBLE TEXT

2 Chronicles 20:15-22

MEMORY VERSE

Psalm 22:3 - "But thou art holy, o thou that inhabitest the praises of Israel."

CENTRAL TRUTH

God enjoys His children showing gratitude to Him.

INTRODUCTION

If people are so particular about those they have helped or provided
for showing gratitude to them, how much more our Maker. We see our parents telling us
from time to time to learn to say thank you for any little thing done or given to us and we
see they are especially happy when we do this without being told or reminded. In fact, they
sometimes give us more or express their joy when we show our gratitude without being told.

In like manner, God likes it when His children acknowledge what He has done in their
lives. As we acknowledge it through gratitude, God is moved to do much more than we think
