ETERNITY IN HEAVEN

LESSON 50

11TH AUGUST

BIBLE TEXT

Luke 23:39-43

MEMORY VERSE

Luke 23:43 - "And Jesus said unto him, Verily I say unto thee, Today shalt thou be with me in paradise."

CENTRAL TRUTH

Prepare for eternity! You must make it to heaven.

OPENING QUESTION: What does heaven mean to you?


INTRODUCTION:
Eternity is a state of timelessness, forever and ever. Heaven is God's
dwelling place. Therefore, our topic for today is about spending eternity with God in
heaven, being with Him forever and ever. Heaven is a place of peace and joy, where
heavenly beings worship God day and night. We cannot comprehend the splendour and
glory of heaven. It is indescribable. See how the streets are described in Rev 21:18-21.

LIFE APPLICATION / INVITATION Think about your eternal home and place your
trust in Christ today before He returns (2 Cor 6:2). Tomorrow may be too late because "if
anyone's name is not found written in the book of life, they will be thrown into the lake of
fire" (Rev 20:15).

CONCLUSION: Every believer must be looking forward to eternity with God. We must
be careful not to allow anything to take our salvation away from us. The Bible says it
profits us nothing if we gain the whole world, yet lose our soul and miss eternity with God
in heaven (Mark 8:36). May this never be our portion, in Jesus' Name. Be grateful to God always!
