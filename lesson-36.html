BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
VICTORY OVER CHALLENGES OF LIFE

LESSON 36

5TH MAY

BIBLE TEXT

Isaiah 41:12-14

MEMORY VERSE

Exodus 14:14 - "The Lord shall fight for you, and ye shall hold your peace

CENTRAL TRUTH

God will fight for you and give you victory if you have a grateful heart.

OPENING QUESTION: Teenagers to mention challenges that people face in life.


INTRODUCTION:
Firstly, let's define some keywords.

CHALLENGE: A challenge can be a problem, difficulty, threat etc. It can also be an
invitation to do something tasking or difficult. Such challenges can interfere with our
daily functioning, quality of life, or even a hindrance to achieving certain goals because
they are issues we can't control. Most times, one would need help to overcome a challenge
-either from man or God. However, at the end of the challenge, when you have overcome
and are completely out of it, you are said to be victorious.


VICTORY: Victory is success, triumph, gaining mastery over something. It is a time
when fear gives way to faith.
