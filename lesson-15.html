TYPES OF GRATITUDE TOWARDS GOD 2:
VAIN WORSHIP

LESSON 15

10TH DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT

1 Samuel 13:4-15

MEMORY VERSE

1 Peter 4:11 - "If any man speak, let him as the oracles of God; if any man minister, let him do it as of the ability which God giveth: that God in all things may be glorified throughs jesus Christ, to whom be praise and dominion for ever and ever. Amen

CENTRAL TRUTH

Vain worship is done to glorify self, not God.

INTRODUCTION

vain means futile, without result, without yield, unfruitful,
unproductive. Vain worship is the act of worshipping God in an unacceptable manner.

STUDY GUIDE

A:)

WHAT IS VAIN WORSHIP?

Vain worship is offering to God an unacceptable sacrifice or worship - Genesis 4:1-7

Vain worship is any worship not offered unto God in Spirit and in truth 4:23-24.

Vain worship is anything offered unto God grudgingly, unwillingly, compulsion - 2 Corinthians 9:7.

Vain worship is worship offered unto God half heartedly with partial obedience. Samuel 15:19-22.

THINGS TO DO IN ORDER NOT TO ALLOW OUR WORSHIP TO BE IN VAIN TOWARDS GOD

1.

Be born again; anyone who is not born again can only offer vain worship to God because he or she is not of God - John 3:3.

2.

Offer unto God only your best; don't give substandard things, don't give money that is torn, faded, squeezed or stolen as an offering to God - 2 Samuel 24:24.

3.

......................................... -john 4:24

4.

Turn away from sinful habits. Don't use the same mouth with which you lie and insult people to worship God. Run away from sinful habits -Ps 66:18

5.

......................................... -john 4:24

6.

Don't hold back or use God's offering for personal things - 1 Sam. 2.29-30.

DISCUSSION: Why are people worshipping God in vain?

LIFE APPLICATION: An unbeliever can only offer vain worship to God. Become a child of
God today by accepting Jesus as your Lord and personal Saviour, then you can ensure
that any worship you offer unto God is done in Spirit and in truth.

CONCLUSION: do you worship and offer gratitude to God? Is it really unto the
Lord or for eye service? God accepts worship that is made genuinely from a pure heart.
Offer acceptable worship to God; acceptable worship can only come from a grateful heart.
