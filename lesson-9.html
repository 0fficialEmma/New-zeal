OUALITIES OF A GRATEFUL HEART 2:
HUMILITY

LESSON 9

29TH OCTOBER

BIBLE TEXT

Daniel 4:35-37

MEMORY VERSE

Daniel 4:37 - "Now 1 Nebuchadnezzar praise and extol and honour the King of heaven, all whose works are truth, and his ways judgment: and those that walk in pride he is able to abase."

CENTRAL TRUTH

It takes humility to express gratitude to God.

INTRODUCTION

A humble heart is such that sees others before himsel for herself and
by so doing, also sees the goodness and kindness of others when expressed to him or her
A proud heart on the other hand, feels entitled to whatever is done or given and as such
sees it as people's responsibility to care or do whatever it is they do for him or her.
Knowing this gives us an understanding of why God resists the proud and gives grace to
the humble (James 4:6).

LIFE APPLICATION:Only a humble person can manifest a grateful heart and
acknowledge the sovereignty of God. We are who we are today through Christ and
We should therefore live in recognition of what God has done and be thankful
continually. We should never feel too big to acknowledge God and be grateful for all
He has done for us.

CONCLUSION: Humility is often characterized as genuine gratitude. True humility is
seeing ourselves as we are, fallen in sin and helpless without God. Through humility, we
develop a grateful heart. However, a life without Christ will always struggle with pride and
cannot achieve humility by strength but with the Help ofGod through the Holy Spirit
