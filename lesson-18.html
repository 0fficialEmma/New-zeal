REASONS TO BE GRATEFUL TO GOD 3:
ETERNAL LIFE IN HEAVEN

LESSON 18

31ST DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT

John 10:28-30

MEMORY VERSE

John 10:28 "And I give unto them eternal life, and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand."

CENTRAL TRUTH

Be grateful to God for the hope of eternal life.

INTRODUCTION

Eternal life is life without end, everlasting life where there will be no
death, sickness or any suffering of any sort. it begins on earth and in heaven. This is
wonderful news to hear, especially with all the tribulations and trials we go through here
on earth.

STUDY GUIDE

WHAT IS ENTERNAL LIFE? JOHN 17:3

Eternal means without end. Eternal life is also the quality of the life lived endlessly. Eternal life is available for only those who believe in Jesus Christ (John 3:16). Eternal life is knowing God.
